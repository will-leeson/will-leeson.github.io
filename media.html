Movie and TV

Movies

   I enjoy movies. I used to be a bit of a snob about it but thats serves no purpose other than to rob other people of their enjoyment. I watch many types of movies (most anything but horror) and enjoy talking about them. I grew up watching a lot of the classics. My Dad showed us Jaws, Star Wars, and many other blockbusters. We also watched all the classic monster movies: The Invisible Man (1933), The Wolfman (1941), and The Blob (1958).
   When I was in high school, I joined the cinema club. Helmed by my physics teacher, I was given a masterclass in cinema. I was exposed to some of my favorite movies, including Ghost World, Metropolitan, and Metropolis. Some of my favorite actors/actresses are Edward Norton, Steve Buscemi, and Saoirse Ronan My favorite directors are Wes Anderson, Quentin Tarantino, and David Fincher.

TV

  Similar to movies, we watched a lot of TV together in my house. Every week day, my dad would come home from work at 4:30. The TV remote would be relinquished to him and, we would watch something until dinner in about an hour. Usually, we would watch classic shows such as M*A*S*H, The Andy Griffith Show, or The Dick Van Dyke Show. I mostly watched comedies and cartoons growing up.
   I started getting into great drama series around the time Netflix became popular, my junior year of high school. The watershed moment for me was watching Twin Peaks. After that, I went back and watched a lot of classic TV dramas.
