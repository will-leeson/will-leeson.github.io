Interests

I am interested in various forms of program analyses. Particularly, I am work with static analysis tools such as ALPACA, KLEE, and IKOS. These tools are incredibly powerful and can analyze code to find potential bugs and errors.

I am also interested in compilers. They were the driving force for my choosing to go to graduate school. Tools like LLVM are beasts that I have foolishly decided to take on. If I can negotiate with it to help me do somethings, I will take what I can get.
