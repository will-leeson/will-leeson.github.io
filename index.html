About

My name is Will Leeson and I am a computer science Ph.D. student at the University of Virginia studying under Matthew Dwyer. I'm originally from the south suburbs of Chicago. I received a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Data Analytics from Drake University. My research interests are program analysis and compiler theory.
